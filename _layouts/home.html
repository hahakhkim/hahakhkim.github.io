Research Interest
My research interests lie in the area of Control, Optimization, and Robotics.
I aim to develop decision-making algorithms that operate safely and efficiently in uncertain or hazardous conditions. I am also interested in addressing practical challenges in robotics, such as safe planning in dynamic environments.

Background
I received B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University with Summa Cum Laude (2020). During undergraduate years, I have been supported by Seoam Scholarship Program and advised by Professor Insoon Yang for my thesis. Before then, I graduated from Seoul Science High School (2014).
