Research Interest
My research interests lie in the area of Control, Optimization, and Robotics.
My goal is to develop decision-making algorithms that operate safely and efficiently in uncertain or hazardous conditions, by employing cutting-edge optimization techniques. I am also interested in addressing practical challenges in robotics, for example, motion planning in dynamic environments, with safety guarantee.
